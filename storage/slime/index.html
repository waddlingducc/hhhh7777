Blumgi Slime

About

In this fun and satisfying game Blumgi Slime, your goal is to control the slime and jump your way to the end! All you can do is jump, but you need to jump at just the right angle and force to either overcome an obstacle, bounce, or dodge a cliff! If you fall into water, than it's game over. Use the walls wisely to bounce and change the slime's direction! This game is even more fun when you're playing in two players, because you get to compete with your friend who reaches the goal first! Have fun!

Controls

  • Space Bar / Click: Jump
  • A: Jump (Player 1)
  • M: Jump (Player 2)
  • R: Restart
