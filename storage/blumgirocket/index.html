About

Blumgi Rocket is a popular and satisfying game where you need to control a rocket-equipped car through all kinds of interesting obstacles and reach the end. Use your driving skills to conquer the unique terrains and use your rocket wisely to shoot through the finish line! Hold the space bar to charge it and use the arrow keys to aim the path of the rocket! Have fun!

Controls

  • Arrow / WASD Keys: Move
  • Space: Charge Rocket
  • R: Restart
